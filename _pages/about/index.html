I am an experienced Machine Learning (ML) Researcher at Qualcomm AI research I have worked extensively on problems such as generative models in vision, on-device efficiency, inverse problems (e.g Object manipulation, Inpainting, RF-sensing, SLAM), Unsupervised models for assisted diagnostic radiology. My current focus lies at the intersection of generative AI (predominantly Diffusion) and efficient on-device models. I am involved in demos, papers and Invention disclosures in link patent, papers, journal.
